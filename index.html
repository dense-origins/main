Common Questions

Frequently asked

What is Fuller's Earth and what is it used for?

Fuller's Earth is a naturally occurring clay mineral with exceptional absorbent and adsorption properties. It is used in cosmetics and skincare (face masks, dry shampoo, talcum powder), pharmaceuticals, agriculture as a pesticide carrier, and in industrial applications such as oil refining and bleaching. Dense Origins supplies cosmetic-grade and industrial-grade Fuller's Earth with full lab certification from India.

What is Psyllium Husk and what is it used for?

Psyllium Husk is the outer seed coating of Plantago ovata, a plant cultivated extensively in India. It is one of the richest natural sources of soluble dietary fibre. It is widely used in functional food and beverages, dietary supplements, pharmaceutical laxative formulations, and nutraceutical products. It is naturally gluten-free, and Dense Origins offers both standard and organic variants.

Where does Dense Origins export to?

Dense Origins exports globally, with established supply to buyers in the European Union, Middle East, North America, and Southeast Asia. We handle all export documentation, logistics, and regulatory compliance to make cross-border procurement seamless for our buyers.

What certifications and documents do you provide?

We provide a Certificate of Analysis (CoA) from accredited third-party laboratories with every batch, along with Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS), phytosanitary certificates for botanical products, and can arrange Halal and Kosher certification on request. All documentation is export-ready.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Dense Origins offers flexible minimum order quantities. We work with startup formulators as well as large-scale manufacturers. Please contact us directly to discuss your volume, grade, and pack specification requirements.

Can you supply custom mesh sizes or grades?

Yes. We can supply Fuller's Earth and Psyllium Husk to your exact mesh size, moisture specification, purity grade, and packaging format — including private label options. Get in touch to discuss your technical requirements.
