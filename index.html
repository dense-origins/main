Product Overview

What is
Fuller's Earth?

Fuller's Earth is a naturally occurring clay mineral composed primarily of palygorskite (attapulgite) or smectite minerals. Its name derives from the historical use of "fulling" wool — a process that used the mineral's extraordinary absorbent power to remove grease and impurities from raw fibres.

Today, it is one of the most versatile industrial and cosmetic raw materials on the planet. Its porous microstructure gives it an exceptionally high surface area, enabling it to physically adsorb oils, pigments, heavy metals, and toxins with remarkable efficiency.

Dense Origins sources Fuller's Earth from high-purity deposits in India, processing it to two grades — cosmetic and industrial — each lab-verified for consistency and compliance.

Applications

Where it's used

💄

Cosmetics & Skincare

A cornerstone ingredient in face masks, dry shampoos, talcum powders, and mineral foundations. Its ability to absorb excess sebum and adsorb impurities makes it ideal for oil-control formulations and detoxifying treatments. Works well across all skin types.

💊

Pharmaceuticals

Used as an excipient, anti-caking agent, and active ingredient in anti-diarrheal preparations and dermatological ointments. Its inert, non-toxic nature and high surface area make it a trusted component in regulated pharmaceutical manufacturing.

Agriculture

Widely used as a carrier and extender for pesticide and herbicide formulations. Its adsorptive properties ensure even distribution of active ingredients, while its natural composition is compatible with organic farming standards.

🛢️

Oil Refining & Bleaching

One of the primary industrial applications — used as a bleaching earth to decolourize and refine edible oils, mineral oils, and lubricants. Removes chlorophyll, carotenoids, soaps, and oxidation products from crude oils.

🏭

Industrial Absorbents

Used in spill-control products, cat litter, and industrial floor absorbents. Its granular form and high liquid-absorption capacity make it an effective and cost-efficient solution for managing industrial spills and waste fluids.

🧪

Chemical & Catalysis

Acts as a catalyst support and filtration aid in various chemical processing applications. Its high surface area and thermal stability are leveraged in petroleum refining and specialty chemical production.

Product Overview

What is
Psyllium Husk?

Psyllium Husk is derived from the outer seed coat (husk) of Plantago ovata, a plant species native to the Indian subcontinent and cultivated predominantly in the states of Gujarat and Rajasthan. India accounts for over 85% of the world's psyllium production.

When hydrated, psyllium husk forms a viscous, gel-like mass due to its remarkable soluble fibre content — primarily arabinoxylan. This gelling property is the basis for its widespread use across food, pharmaceutical, and nutraceutical applications globally.

Dense Origins supplies psyllium husk processed to exacting moisture, husk purity, and swell index specifications — available in standard food grade and certified organic variants.

Applications

Where it's used

🥣

Functional Foods & Beverages

Added to breakfast cereals, nutrition bars, breads, and health drinks as a fibre-enriching ingredient. Its neutral taste and high water-absorption make it ideal for high-fibre product ranges without affecting flavour profiles.

💊

Dietary Supplements

The primary ingredient in fibre supplement powders, capsules, and sachets sold globally. Known for its efficacy in promoting regularity, supporting satiety, and managing healthy blood glucose and cholesterol levels.

🏥

Pharmaceutical Formulations

Used as both an active ingredient and excipient in OTC laxatives, bulk-forming laxatives, and constipation-management products. Widely referenced in international pharmacopoeias for its safety and efficacy profile.

🧬

Nutraceuticals

Featured in gut-health formulations, prebiotic supplements, and metabolic health products. Scientific evidence supports its role in cholesterol management, blood sugar regulation, and microbiome support.

🧁

Gluten-Free Baking

Acts as a natural binder and texturiser in gluten-free baked goods, replacing the structural role of gluten. Creates the elasticity and moisture retention that gluten-free flours typically lack.

🐾

Pet Nutrition

Incorporated into premium pet food formulations and veterinary fibre supplements for digestive health management in dogs and cats. An increasingly important ingredient in the fast-growing functional pet food segment.
